Even tought Coffee and Flowers industry are the most exported productive chain in Colombia, the production chain of Cotton could be more profitable.

By Edison Alexis Suarez Ducon

That's right, the production chain of the cotton, textiles and clothing industry has a greater margin of value in relation to price per ton. While the price per tonne in coffee was USD$3,493.57 on average in 2016, in cotton was USD$11,895.61 on average (3.4 times more). The ratio of price per ton follows the flowers, with $ 6,040.38 per ton, and the Cacao at $ 4,821.93.
